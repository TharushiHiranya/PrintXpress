# User guide

This guide shows how to use PrintXpress to order printed items and track them. It is written for customers. It covers Task F of the assignment, the user documentation.

## What you need

A phone running Android 7.0 or later. The app works fully offline, so you do not need an internet connection to browse or place an order.

## Install the app

1. Copy the PrintXpress APK to your phone, or install it from Android Studio onto a connected phone or emulator.
2. If the phone asks, allow installing from this source.
3. Open the app once it is installed.

## Create an account

1. On the welcome screen, tap Register.
2. Enter your full name.
3. Enter your email or your phone number.
4. Choose a password of at least six characters.
5. Tap Register. You are taken to the home screen, already logged in.

Your password is stored in a scrambled form, so it is never kept as plain text.

## Log in

1. On the welcome screen, tap Log in.
2. Enter the email or phone you registered with, and your password.
3. Tap Log in. You stay logged in until you log out, even after closing the app.

## Browse products

The home screen shows the product categories, such as business cards, posters, banners, flyers, stickers, mugs, and t-shirts.

1. Tap a category to see its products.
2. Tap a product to see its price, material, sample image, and the available sizes and options.
3. Use the filter on the product list to narrow it by type, material, or size.

## Place an order

1. Open the product you want.
2. Choose the quantity, the material or paper, and the size. The price updates as you choose.
3. Add your design in one of two ways. Either tap Upload to pick an image or PDF from your phone, or type your text in the custom text box.
4. Choose pickup or delivery. For delivery, pick one of your saved addresses.
5. Review the summary, then tap Place order.
6. The confirmation screen shows your order number and a summary. You also get a confirmation notification.

## Save a design to reuse

1. After uploading a design, tap Save design and give it a title.
2. The saved design appears in your profile.
3. On a later order, pick it from your saved designs instead of uploading again.

## Track your orders

1. Open the Orders screen.
2. Each order shows its date, total, and status. Status moves through processing, printing, ready for pickup or out for delivery, and completed.
3. Tap an order to see its items and full status.

## Change or cancel an order

You can cancel or reschedule only while an order is still processing. Once printing starts, these options are turned off.

1. Open the order from the Orders screen.
2. Tap Cancel to cancel it, or Reschedule to change it.
3. A cancelled order shows the cancelled status and cannot be edited.

## Notifications and offers

1. Open the Notifications screen to see updates, such as order confirmations and completions. Unread items have a marker, and tapping one marks it read.
2. Open the Offers screen or the home banner to see current discounts. Each offer shows its title, discount, code, and valid dates. Expired offers do not appear.

## Manage your profile and addresses

1. Open the Profile screen.
2. Edit your name, email, or phone, then save.
3. Under addresses, add, edit, or delete a delivery address, and set one as your default for faster checkout.

## Print guidelines

The Guidelines screen explains how to prepare a file for good print quality, including accepted file types, the resolution to aim for, the colour mode, and bleed margins. Read it before uploading a design to avoid delays.

## FAQs and support

The FAQ screen answers common questions about ordering and design. For help with a design question, use the contact details shown on the FAQ screen to reach the shop.

## Troubleshooting

| Problem | What to do |
|---------|-----------|
| The app will not let me register | Check the email has an at sign, the phone is digits only, and the password is at least six characters. |
| It says my email is already used | That email has an account. Log in instead, or use a different email. |
| I cannot add an item to my order | Add a design file or type custom text first, then set a quantity, size, and material. |
| I cannot cancel my order | Cancel is only available while the order is processing. Once printing starts it is locked. |
| Delivery will not go through | Pick a delivery address, or add one in your profile first. |
